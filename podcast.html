The Podcast

Split:A Podcast on Modern Divorce.

Hosted by renowned family law attorney Gus Dimopoulos, Split: A Podcast on Modern Divorce explores the complex realities of divorce, custody, parental alienation, coercive control, mental health, and modern family conflict through candid, insightful conversations.

Blending legal expertise with real-world perspective, Gus sits down with attorneys, therapists, experts, advocates, and individuals with lived experience to unpack the emotional, psychological, and legal challenges families face during separation and custody disputes.

Raw, honest, and deeply informative, Split has become a trusted resource for anyone navigating divorce or trying to better understand the evolving dynamics of modern family law.
