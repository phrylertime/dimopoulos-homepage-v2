Kathleen “Kassie” Seavy

Associate

Matrimonial & Family Law
Credentials

Pace Law — summa cum laude, 2024 · Federal Judicial Honors Program

Kathleen “Kassie” Seavy is an associate at Dimopoulos Law Firm, P.C., where she focuses her practice on matrimonial and family law litigation. Before joining the firm full time, Ms. Seavy held federal internships in the chambers of the Hon. Philip M. Halpern of the United States District Court for the Southern District of New York and at the United States Attorney’s Office for the Southern District of New York, Criminal Division.

Ms. Seavy earned her Juris Doctor, summa cum laude, from the Elisabeth Haub School of Law at Pace University in May 2024 (merit scholarship), graduating near the top of her class. At Pace, she served as Case Note Editor of the Pace Environmental Law Review and was selected for the Federal Judicial Honors Program.
