Hannah Gaudet

Associate

Matrimonial & Family Law
Credentials

Pace Law — 2025 · Pace Women’s Justice Center extern

Hannah Gaudet is an associate at Dimopoulos Law Firm, P.C., where she focuses her practice on matrimonial and family law litigation. She supports the firm across the lifecycle of a contested matter — drafting motion papers, conducting discovery; conducting legal research; managing case files; and appearing with the firm at conferences and proceedings in the Westchester courts.

Before joining the firm, Ms. Gaudet served as a legal extern at the Pace Women’s Justice Center, where she advocated for domestic violence survivors in Westchester Family Court — interviewing clients, gathering evidence, and drafting petitions for temporary orders of protection and custody. The work gave her early, direct exposure to the procedural rhythms of the courts in which the firm appears every week.

Ms. Gaudet earned her Juris Doctor from the Elisabeth Haub School of Law at Pace University in 2025. At Pace, she served as Acquisitions Editor of the Pace Environmental Law Review, Fundraising Co-Chair of the school’s team in the Jeffrey G. Miller National Environmental Law Moot Court Competition, and Editor-in-Chief of the Hearsay student newspaper, and was an active member of the Family Law Society.
