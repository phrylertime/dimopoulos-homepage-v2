Attorney Profile

Gus Dimopoulos

Managing Partner

Matrimonial · Custody · High-Conflict
A peer review

“A go-to attorney for the most complicated divorce cases in the New York region.”

— Peer review — matrimonial bar

Gus Dimopoulos, Esq., managing partner of Dimopoulos Law Firm PC, has received nearly every award a matrimonial attorney can. He has been named a Super Lawyer by Thomson Reuters annually since 2019 in the field of matrimonial law — an honor bestowed upon fewer than five percent of the attorneys in the country — and has earned recognition as a Lawyer of Distinction in Family Law.

Mr. Dimopoulos has been selected for membership in Premier Lawyers of America, awarded “10 Best” by the American Institute of Family Law Attorneys, and repeatedly received Avvo.com’s Client Choice Award where he maintains a 10 out of 10 client satisfaction score. His insights have been featured in Accounting Today, TaxStringer, and Authority Magazine, among others.

“Empathetic, patient when I needed something explained, strong when I felt weak — and my prize fighter in the ring.”
— Lisa · Divorce, Westchester

Widely recognized as a go-to attorney for the most complicated divorce cases, Mr. Dimopoulos has handled extremely contentious custody matters, consistently securing custody on behalf of his clients when no settlement options were available. His familiarity and study of mental illness, forensic evaluations, narcissistic personality disorder, coercive control, and drug and alcohol issues help him resolve even the most difficult cases.

Beyond the courtroom, Gus hosts Split: A Podcast on Modern Divorce, a long-form conversation series that gives clients clarity and perspective on the realities of separation, custody, and life after marriage. His thought leadership extends across social media and national publications, where he is regularly quoted on changes in matrimonial law and the human side of family conflict.

Notable matters

A small sample of cases Gus has handled.

Matters are described in general terms to preserve client confidentiality. Specific outcomes can be discussed in a consultation.

  1. 01Secured sole legal and physical custody on behalf of a parent in a high-conflict matter involving substance abuse and coercive control, after a multi-week trial in Westchester County Supreme Court.
  2. 02Successfully defended a client against unfounded abuse allegations made in the course of a divorce, securing a full dismissal of the petition and a finding of bad-faith pleading against the petitioner.
  3. 03Negotiated a confidential nine-figure separation agreement for a financial-services executive, resolving valuation disputes over closely-held business interests, restricted stock, and deferred compensation without trial.
  4. 04Obtained a contested parental-relocation order permitting a custodial parent to move from Westchester to another state with the children, over the objection of the non-custodial parent.
  5. 05Represented a public-figure client through a high-discretion divorce involving multi-jurisdictional assets, securing a confidential resolution and complete press containment.
