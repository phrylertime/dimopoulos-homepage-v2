How long does a divorce in New York take?
An uncontested divorce can be finalized in a few months; contested matters — especially involving complex assets or custody — can run a year or more. We work to keep your matter on the shortest defensible timeline.
Do you handle cases outside Westchester?
Yes. The firm regularly practices in New York City, Putnam, Dutchess, Rockland, and Orange Counties.
What does an initial consultation cost?
We offer a paid initial consultation. We will explain our fee structure clearly before any work begins.
Will my matter be handled discreetly?
Discretion is foundational to our practice. We routinely represent professionals, executives, and public figures whose privacy is non-negotiable.
